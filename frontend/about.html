About us




      “JNA Sneakers Store”. It is the acronym of the three founder; Johan, Najib and Aqeel. On the 23rd of August 2020, our founders have decided to start this business after having a lot of hassle when shopping for sneakers. JNA Sneakers Store isn’t just about what you wear, but also a projection of our personal lifestyle, culture and homeland.

      Our shoes pay homage to the things we find familiar whilst growing up in South East Asia. This approach makes it even more personal for us; our geography, through our eyes. The most ordinary and mundane things sometimes defines who we really are, but more importantly, where we come from.
